## Discussion Summary
Concise summary of the structured multi-reviewer discussion. Do not include
raw chain-of-thought. Capture: which reviewers challenged which claims,
what evidence was requested, and how each dispute was resolved or escalated.

## Agreements

## Disagreements

- Claim in dispute
- Reviewers in disagreement
- Evidence gap or conflicting interpretation
- Proposed resolution path
